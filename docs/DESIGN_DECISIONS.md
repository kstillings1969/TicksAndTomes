
# Design Decisions (v5)

- Experience-based progression (no ages, eras, or portals)
- Tick-based time economy
- Skills capped at level 5 with streak decay
- Spell Shield retained (spell effects only)
- Tome Towers provide 100 defense per fully staffed tower (100 tomes)
- Focus on economy, PvP, clans, and market-driven net worth growth
